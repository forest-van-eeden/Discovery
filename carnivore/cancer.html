Organic Decay Consumption

Plants are the natural and organic species to consume decay matter. These forms of matter include any form of dead bodies such as, dead animals, dead humans, dead insects, dead fish, dead mammals, dead reptiles, or any other form of dead flesh.

The practice of eating meat began in times of famine, when no plants were remaining to eat. From there, sacrificial cultures began to eat meat in order to appeal to some form of higher power, creating delicacy within meat eating practices, worshiping the practice itself. Since then, meat eating has become a normalised part of human culture and decreed as the only food for their cats and dogs.
