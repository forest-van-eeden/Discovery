Infinity

Outside of Boem, we are inside of Infinity, a being like a dragon of infinite reality. Infinity had an injury occur to him. He was bitten by another of his kind, having a sort of disease like Rabies. Where we are in Infinity was torn off of his body, his tail to be exact.

These things came to be known to me after I painted this, below.

What I was painting was an act of worship, expressing my feelings to and about God by painting, simply, God's body. Once my dot series was impressed, I stood back at the collage, having hung it on the corner of my wall, fitting perfectly there, and I witnessed what was the actuality of our existence here, Infinity's injury. It was so like a map that I had painted.

After further questioning to the Heavenly bodies, it was confirmed to me to be true. The corner within the body of Infinity, in the painting, is where we are.
